Vegetable Biryani

Description

Vegetable Biryani is an all-time favourite rice delicay, now famous all around the world.

A madley of vegetables and rice, cooked with spice powders, and a masaladar paste of coconut, ginger, garlic, and green chilly. It is quite easy to make in the microwave oven and needs simple accomplishments like curds or raita.

Ingredients

  • Couliflower florets
  • Choped carrots
  • Potato cubes
  • Green peas
  • Choped onions
  • Choped coriander
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala salt
  • Sliced onions
  • Basmati rice
  • Oil
  • Choped ginger
  • Choped garlic
  • Choped green chillies

Steps

  1. Combine the cauliflower, carrot, potatoes, green peas, onions, coriander, turmeric powder, coriander-cumin seeds powder, garam masala, the prepared paste, salt and 1¼ cups of water, in a big microwave-safe bowl, mix well and microwave on high for 20 minutes, while stirring once after 10 minutes.
  2. Combine 1 tbsp of oil and sliced onions in another microwave-safe bowl, mix well and microwave on high for 7 minutes. Divide the onions into 2 equal portions and keep aide.
  3. Spread a portion of the onions evenly on a 175 mm. (7”) diameter deep microwave-safe bowl and spread 1 cup of the rice evenly over it.
  4. Spread the prepared vegetable mixture evenly over it and again spread the remaining 1½ cups of rice evenly over it.
  5. Finally spread the other portion of the fried onions and sprinkle the coriander evenly over it and microwave on high for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
